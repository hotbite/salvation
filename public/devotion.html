Today's Devotion

Trusting God in Difficult Times

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding."
Proverbs 3:5

When life becomes difficult, it is easy to rely on our own strength. But God calls us to trust Him completely. Faith grows when we surrender our worries and place our confidence in Him.
